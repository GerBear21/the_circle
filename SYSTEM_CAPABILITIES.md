# The Circle — System Capabilities

## Forms & Request Types

| # | Form / Module | Description |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | **CAPEX Automation** | Automates capital expenditure requests, multi-level approvals, and full lifecycle tracking from submission through to funding completion. |
| 2 | **Travel Authorization** | Streamlines local and international travel requests with automated routing, compliance checks, and HR cost-centre allocation at the final approval step. |
| 3 | **Hotel Complimentaries** | Manages complimentary accommodation bookings for internal staff with structured approval chains and robust booking tracking. |
| 4 | **External Complimentary Vouchers** | Automates the issuance and tracking of accommodation vouchers for external partners and guests, ensuring full transparency and accountability. |
| 5 | **Petty Cash Requests** | Digitises petty cash disbursement requests with defined approval chains, cost-centre allocation, and a complete audit trail per transaction. |
| 6 | **Debit / Credit Notes** | Streamlines the creation and approval of financial adjustment notes with authorised signatory capture and a full, timestamped audit record. |
| 7 | **Journal Entries** | Manages journal entry requests through departmental review and finance sign-off, replacing manual paper-based submissions with a governed digital process. |
| 8 | **General Ledger Postings** | Facilitates GL posting requests with mandatory finance approval, classification tracking, and supporting document attachment before processing. |
| 9 | **Voucher Requests** | Automates cost-centre voucher issuance and approval, ensuring every disbursement is fully authorised and traceable before it is actioned. |
| 10 | **Custom Forms Digitization** | Enables any department to digitise an existing paper-based form into a structured, validated digital workflow — without requiring code changes. |

## Platform Features & Administration

| # | Feature | Description |
|---|---|---|
| 11 | **Workflow & Form Builder** | Administrators can design custom approval chains and form templates from scratch. All new or modified workflows require admin review and approval before they go live. |
| 12 | **Multi-Step Approval Engine** | Supports both sequential and parallel approval chains with conditional step logic, dynamic approver resolution (by role, department, or organogram position), and built-in delegation handling. |
| 13 | **Risk-Based Authentication** | Automatically escalates the authentication requirement — from session confirmation to Microsoft MFA to biometric (WebAuthn) — based on monetary value, department sensitivity, and position in the approval chain. |
| 14 | **Electronic Signatures** | Captures a legally attributable signature (saved, hand-drawn, or typed) from every approver at the point of decision, embedded directly into the final approval archive. |
| 15 | **Approval Delegation** | Allows approvers to formally delegate their authority to a designated colleague for a defined date range; all delegations are audited and time-bounded. |
| 16 | **Role-Based Access Control (RBAC)** | Enforces granular, scoped permissions across every system action. Role assignments can be limited to a specific department or business unit and may carry an expiry date. |
| 17 | **Real-Time Notifications** | Delivers instant in-app and email alerts to approvers and requesters at every workflow stage transition, keeping all parties informed without manual follow-up. |
| 18 | **PDF Archive & Audit Trail** | Automatically generates a tamper-evident PDF record upon workflow completion, capturing all decisions, signatures, authentication methods, device metadata, and timestamps in a single document. |
| 19 | **CAPEX Tracker** | Tracks approved capital expenditure requests through the post-approval funding lifecycle — from awaiting funding through to funding complete — with real-time status visibility. |
| 20 | **Dashboard & Reporting** | Provides real-time KPI metrics, SLA compliance tracking, and request analytics, giving management a live overview of workflow performance and bottlenecks. |
